I am Yafet Tedla, vice president of the Safari Academy Space Science Club (SASSC). History is a solid proof that every great scientist and deed begins in a small community school like ours. I believe our space science club will unlock some potential that will bring a bright future upon this country and even the world.

With stargazing sessions, cosmic simulations, and collaborative projects, we foster a community of cosmic adventurers. We share the tales of astronauts, the wonders of black holes, and the awe-inspiring beauty of nebulae, all in the pursuit of unveiling the universe's secrets. Our story is one of curiosity, learning, and a shared fascination for the infinite cosmos.
